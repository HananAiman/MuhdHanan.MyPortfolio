BMI Calculator System

Description

Body Mass Index, commonly known as BMI, is a numerical representation derived from an individual's height and weight. Utilizing BMI allows us to assess the health implications of an individual's weight. The formula for BMI calculation is straightforward:

BMI = Weight in Kilograms / (Height in Meters * Height in Meters)

Individuals are classified into categories such as Underweight, Normal, Overweight, or Obese based on their BMI falling within specific ranges. This categorization provides a quick and informative way to gauge weight-related health status.:
